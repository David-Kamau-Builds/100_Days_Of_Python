The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time

1. Spirited Away

Genre: Animation, Fantasy

This is my favourite anime. I love the beautiful images and the magical storytelling that transports you to another world.

2. Ex Machina

Genre: Science Fiction, Thriller

Really cool sci-fi movie that explores artificial intelligence and what it means to be human. The cinematography is stunning.

3. Drive

Genre: Action, Drama

Super beautiful film. Really artistic with an amazing soundtrack and incredible visual style that stays with you long after watching.
