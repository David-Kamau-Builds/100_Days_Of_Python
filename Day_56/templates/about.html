About 1 Second Gap

Your ultimate destination for Formula 1 memes, hilarious moments, and funny GIFs from the world of motorsport.

What is 1 Second Gap?

1 Second Gap is a fan-made community dedicated to celebrating the funny side of Formula 1. Named after the often-discussed gap between drivers, our mission is to bring laughter and entertainment to F1 fans worldwide. We curate the best memes, GIFs, and hilarious moments from the sport.

Our Mission

We believe that F1 is not just about high-speed racing and technical excellence—it's also about the personalities, drama, and humor that make the sport captivating. Our goal is to create a fun space where fans can share and enjoy the lighter side of Formula 1.

Community

We celebrate the global F1 community. Whether you're a hardcore fan who watches every race or a casual viewer who enjoys the memes, there's something for everyone on 1 Second Gap.
